Fluke

Find your 'Music Mate'

View on GitHub Download .zip Download .tar.gz

“Music is the universal language of mankind.” When we meet our other half we generally call them our soulmate. But what happens when you meet someone over the music that you love? Fluke allows you to listen to your favourite song and search for other strangers who are listening to the same song from all around the world. Fluke is compatible with Google play music so that it is ensured that you can switch between songs and your playlists seamlessly. It's light, it's beautiful and it's mesmerizing. Ready? now go search for your 'music mate'.
